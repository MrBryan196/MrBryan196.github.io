Naruto Shippuden

Naruto Shippuden is the anime adaption of part 2 of the manga Naruto. The earlier anime series Naruto adapted the first part of the manga. Like it's predecessor it is set in the fantasy environment known as The Ninja World and focuses on characters from Konoha (The Village Hidden in the Leaves.

Naruto Shippuden is set 2 and a half years after the earlier series and continues to follow the lives and adventures of the ninjas Naruto, Sasuke, Sakura and their sensei Kakashi. The young ninja are 16 years old by the start of Shippuden.

The main plotline deals with the threat facing Konoha and the Ninja World posed by the mysterious group of rogue ninja the Akatsuki.
